Chicken Gumbo

The best gumbo I've ever had
Prep: 60 minutes
Cook: 180 minutes
Ingredients
    1 lb chicken thighs 1 lb smoked andouille sausage 1 med onion 1 bell pepper 1 jalapeno 2 ribs celery 6-8 garlic cloves 1 qt chicken broth 10 oz beer 5 oz high heat oil 1/2 cup flour 5 bay leaves 1 tsp fresh thyme salt pepper
Instructions
  •   1. Sear chicken with about 1 oz oil and salt and pepper to taste for about 8 minutes each side on medium-high heat until golden brown.
  •   2. De-glaze pan with about 2oz beer and set aside for gumbo.
  •   3. Cut sausage into bite size pieces.
  •   4. Dice onion, bell pepper, jalapeno, and celery.
  •   5. Mince Garlic - keep separate.
  •   6. While chicken is cooking heat 4oz oil on low-medium heat.
  •   7. Once the oil starts smoking add Â½ cup flour - stirring continuously until a chocolate brown - 10-30 minutes depending on heat.
  •   8. Add onion, bell pepper, jalapeno, celery and a pinch of salt.
  •   9. Sweat vegetables for a few minutes.
  •   10. Add garlic.
  •   11. Cook for a few more minutes.
  •   12. Add 8oz beer - cook out alcohol stirring continuously.
  •   13. Slowly add 1 Qt. chicken stock while stirring.
  •   14. Add chicken and all pan juices, sausage, 5 bay leaves, 1 tsp. fresh thyme, 1 tbs. salt, and 1 tbs. Pepper.
  •   15. Bring to a low boil.
  •   16. Reduce heat and simmer for at least 3 hours stirring about every 15 minutes.
