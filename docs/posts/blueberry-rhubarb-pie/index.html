Blueberry Rhubarb Pie

Ingredients
    1/4 tsp Salt 1/4 cup Brown sugar 1 cup Sugar 1/4 cup Quick cooking tapioca 3 cups Diced rhubarb 3 cups Blueberries 2 Pie dough
Instructions
  •   1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  •   2. Stir in sugar, brown sugar, tapioca and salt together in a large bowl.
  •   3. Add rhubarb and blueberries; toss to coat.
  •   4. Place 1 pie dough in the bottom of pie plate.
  •   5. Pile rhubarb-blueberry mixture on top of dough.
  •   6. Place second pie dough on top.
  •   7. Crimp crust together.
  •   8. Bake for 20 minutes.
  •   9. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and continue baking until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling, 25 to 30 minutes more.
  •   10. Cool for 2 hours before serving.
