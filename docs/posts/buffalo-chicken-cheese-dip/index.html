Buffalo Chicken Cheese Dip

Ingredients
    5 cups Shredded chicken 4 cups Mozzarella cheese 12 oz Cream cheese 2 cups Cheddar cheese 2 cups Monterey jack cheese 1 cup Blue cheese 12 oz Red hot buffalo sauce
Instructions
  •   1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  •   2. Cook chicken breasts until done, then shred up completely.
  •   3. Combine cream cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, red hot buffalo sauce and chicken into a bowl and mix well.
  •   4. Put in oven safe dish.
  •   5. Put shredded cheddar cheese, shredded monterey jack cheese and crumbled blue cheese on top of chicken mixture.
  •   6. Bake until cheese on top is bubbly.
