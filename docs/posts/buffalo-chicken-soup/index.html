Buffalo Chicken Soup

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 10 minutes
Ingredients
    2 Chicken breasts 3 cups Chicken broth 5 stocks Diced celery 1/2 Diced onion 1 tablespoon Minced garlic 1 tablespoon Ranch dressing mix 1/3 cup Frank's red hot 2 cups Shredded cheddar cheese 2 tablespoons Butter 1 cup Heavy cream
Instructions
  •   1. In your pressure cooker combine all ingredients EXCEPT cream & cheese.
  •   2. Cook under pressure for 10 minutes then quick depressurize.
  •   3. Carefully remove chicken, shred and return to soup. Add heavy cream and cheese, stir to combine.
