Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes

Ingredients
    3 cups Milk 6 tbsp Flour 2 tsp Salt 6 tbsp Butter 4 cups Thinly sliced potatoes Cayenne 3 cups Sharp cheddar cheese Paprika
Instructions
  •   1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  •   2. In a sauce pan, melt butter and blend in flour.
  •   3. Let sit a minute, then dd the cold milk, stirring with a whisk.
  •   4. Season with salt and cayenne.
  •   5. Cook sauce on low until smooth and boiling, stirring occasionally with a whisk.
  •   6. Reduce heat and stir in 2 cups of cheese.
  •   7. Place half of the sliced potatoes in a lightly greased casserole dish.
  •   8. Pour half the cheese sauce over potatoes.
  •   9. Repeat with second layer of potatoes and cheese sauce.
  •   10. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top.
  •   11. Top with some paprika for color.
  •   12. Bake uncovered for an hour.
