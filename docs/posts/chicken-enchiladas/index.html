Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients
    Salt Pepper Vegetable oil Chicken breasts Cream cheese Cheddar cheese Flour tortillas Sour cream Salsa Red enchilada sauce Diced green chilis
Instructions
  •   1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  •   2. (all ingredient measurements are estimations.)
  •   3. Cook chicken until done.
  •   4. Season with salt and pepper.
  •   5. Shred chicken with forks.
  •   6. In a non-stick pan, heat vegetable oil and coat tortillas in it. Set aside.
  •   7. In a bowl, mix sour cream, shredded cheddar cheese, cream cheese, salsa, a little enchilada sauce, green chilis, and shredded chicken. Mix together.
  •   8. Dip tortillas in enchilada sauce on both sides.
  •   9. Add filling, roll up and put into a pan.
  •   10. Pour rest of enchilada sauce on top.
  •   11. Bake covered for 25 minutes.
  •   12. Uncover and put cheese on top and bake for another 10 minutes.
