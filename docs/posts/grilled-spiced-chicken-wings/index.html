Grilled Spiced Chicken Wings

Cook: 30 minutes
Ingredients
    3 tbsp Butter 8 Chicken wings 1 tsp Olive oil 2 tsp Seafood seasoning
Instructions
  •   1. Heat grill to medium
  •   2. In a large bowl, toss the wings with the oil and 1 teaspoon of the seafood seasoning.
  •   3. Grill, uncovered, turning once, until cooked through, 15 to 20 minutes.
  •   4. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine the butter and the remaining teaspoon of seafood seasoning.
  •   5. Add the wings and toss to combine.
  •   6. Serve with ranch dressing for dipping.
