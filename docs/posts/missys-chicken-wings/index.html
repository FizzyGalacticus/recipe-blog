Missy's Chicken Wings

Ingredients
    4 oz Butter 10 lbs Chicken wings 23 oz Red hot 24 oz Tobasco sauce
Instructions
  •   1. Heat fryer up to 375 degrees.
  •   2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  •   3. In a large pot, put Red Hot, Tabasco, and butter on medium heat.
  •   4. Cook chicken wings in batches in fryer for 10 minutes each.
  •   5. Pull out of fryer and place in sauce submerging them completely.
  •   6. Place in oven on a cookie sheet to keep warm while other batches of chicken wings are being cooked.
